How to Protect Your Website from Hackers

Security is the most overlooked part of web building. At Romankind, we believe every site—no matter how small—needs a solid defense strategy.

Rule #1: SSL Certificates. Never launch a site without "HTTPS." Google will flag your site as "Not Secure," hurting your traffic and trust.

Top Security Tips:

  • Use Strong Passwords: Avoid "admin123." Use a password manager.
  • Keep Software Updated: Whether you use WordPress or a custom Vercel build, keep your libraries updated.
  • Limit Login Attempts: Prevent "Brute Force" attacks by blocking IPs that fail to login 3 times.
